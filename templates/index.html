Welcome to Fürstenhof Equestrian

Nestled amidst the picturesque landscapes of East Frisia, Fürstenhof stands as a sanctuary dedicated to the holistic care and training of our cherished equine companions. Our unwavering commitment to their well-being knows no bounds, transcending the boundaries between English dressage and Western horsemanship. At Fürstenhof, it's not merely a passion; it's a way of life.

You can find out more about us on our website and feel free to send us a message. In the logged in area there is more information about the horses and their origins. Simply Register and Login and you're good to go


Our Trainer

At Fürstenhof, our dedicated trainer takes meticulous care of every stage in the development of young horses, starting from foals and guiding them through a comprehensive training program that extends to the most advanced levels. Our seasoned professional ensures that every horse reaches its full potential, mastering the intricacies of each class with a depth of expertise that is second to none


Our ridetrack

Under the enchanting night sky is our illuminated riding arena with a spacious area of 60 x 20 meters. Bathed in a soft, enchanting light that comes to life as daylight fades. Here riders and their horses become one to create something truly enchanting. The generous lighting transforms every movement into a graceful ballet, be it the rhythmic trot, the spirited gallop or the thrill of the piaffe. But it's not just a picturesque sight; It is a place of joyful exploration and learning. With the different lessons, every training session is full of excitement. No matter whether it is a young horse for the first time under saddle or an experienced horse on the way to the Grand Prix. Our riding arena is like a spacious playground where the horses are well looked after. Thanks to its generous space, it even offers space for several riders at the same time.




The summer pasture

During the sun-kissed months from May to October, our enchanting summer pasture transforms into a sanctuary for our beloved equines. Here, two distinct herds, one consisting of nurturing mares with their playful foals, and the other comprised of spirited geldings and Stallions, roam freely across approximately 3 hectares of lush, verdant terrain. Within this idyllic setting, our horses have the opportunity to luxuriate in the expanse, reveling in their natural expressions. Our dedicated team maintains a vigilant watch over these equine herds. Each morning, the horses are released to graze in the pastures, and they return to the stables for their evening meal. Come rain or shine, we are unwavering in our commitment to ensuring their every need is met. The ever-changing weather may dictate the duration of our time in this equine paradise, yet whether the summer stretches its embrace long or briefly, one thing remains constant—the devotion to our horses' well-being.


Our Winter paddock

Even in the harshest of winters, our majestic equines roam freely when nature allows. Within the expanse of our winter paddocks, they find an endless supply of fresh, fragrant hay and crystal-clear water throughout the day and night. In the realm of Fürstenhof, the changing seasons only serve to further reveal the indomitable spirit and resilience of our cherished companions.


The stable

We have lovingly transformed a rustic cowshed into a haven of warmth and comfort for our equine companions. Within these spacious, individual stalls, every horse finds solace and tranquility, allowing them to rejuvenate after a day of dedicated effort. At Fürstenhof, our commitment to their well-being knows no bounds, and it's evident in every cozy corner of our stable.
